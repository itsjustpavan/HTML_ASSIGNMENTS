Recipe to Make FRENCH OMELET


INGREDIENTS

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp of water
  • 1/8 tbsp of salt
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • cheese

PROCEDURE

  1. BEAT eggs, water, salt and pepper in small bowl until blended.
  2. Place a 10-in. skillet coated with cooking spray over medium heat.
  3. Pour in egg mixture. Mixture should set immediately at edges.
  4. When eggs are thickened and no liquid egg remains, top one side with remaining ingredients.
